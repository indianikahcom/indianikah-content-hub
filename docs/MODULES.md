# Modules

## 1. Dashboard

Central place for monitoring everything.

---

## 2. Content Sources

Reads content from:

- Marriage Guidelines
- Books
- Videos
- News
- Scholarships
- Haj & Umrah Guide
- Matrimony Profile Insights

---

## 3. AI Engine

Generates:

- Captions
- Summaries
- Hashtags
- Post titles
- Image prompts

---

## 4. Approval Queue

Administrator reviews every post before publishing.

---

## 5. Publishing Engine

Publishes approved posts to:

- Facebook
- Instagram
- X
- LinkedIn
- Telegram
- WhatsApp Channels (future)

---

## 6. Scheduler

Plans daily publishing schedule.

---

## 7. Analytics

Tracks:

- Published posts
- Clicks
- Engagement
- Traffic

---

## 8. Settings

Manage:

- API Keys
- Social accounts
- Prompt templates
- Publishing schedule

---

## 9. Logs

Track:

- AI requests
- Publishing history
- Errors

---

## 10. Content Editor

A rich editor for creating and managing manual posts with media attachments, previews, scheduling, and publishing controls.
