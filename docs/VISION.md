# IndiaNikah AI Content Hub

## Vision

IndiaNikah AI Content Hub is a standalone application that helps create, manage, approve, and publish high-quality content across multiple social media platforms.

The application uses AI together with data from IndiaNikah to generate educational, informative, and engaging content while ensuring complete human approval before publishing.

Its primary objective is to increase awareness of IndiaNikah, educate the Muslim community about marriage and family life, and consistently drive visitors back to the IndiaNikah website.

---

## Problem Statement

IndiaNikah contains valuable content including matrimony profiles, marriage guidelines, books, videos, news, scholarships, and Haj/Umrah guides.

Currently, this content is not promoted consistently across social media, resulting in missed opportunities to reach new audiences and increase engagement.

Creating content manually every day is time-consuming.

---

## Why are we building this?

We want a system that can:

- Discover useful content automatically
- Generate AI-assisted social media posts
- Produce anonymized profile insights
- Recommend videos, books, and news
- Require manual approval before publishing
- Publish to multiple social media platforms
- Save time while maintaining content quality

---

## Goals

- Increase IndiaNikah website traffic.
- Increase user registrations.
- Build trust in the IndiaNikah brand.
- Educate Muslims about healthy marriages and family life.
- Publish consistent, high-quality content every day.
- Maintain complete human control before publishing.

---

## Non Goals

- Not replacing the IndiaNikah website.
- Not modifying the production database.
- Not exposing personal profile information.
- Not publishing automatically without approval.

---

## Target Users

Primary User:

- IndiaNikah Administrator

Future Users:

- Content Managers
- Marketing Team
- Editors

---

## Success Metrics

- Increased social media engagement.
- Increased website traffic from social media.
- Increased user registrations.
- Reduced manual effort for content creation.
- Daily content published consistently.
